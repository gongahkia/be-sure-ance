# ADR 0003: Position As Qualitative Research Complement To compareFIRST

## Status

Accepted.

## Context

compareFIRST is the regulated quantitative comparison reference for applicable Singapore life-insurance products. Lead-generation aggregators already compete for consumer quote and funnel traffic.

The defensible gap for Be-sure-ance is source-traceable qualitative metadata: panel/network clues, exclusions, waiting periods, claim-process text, brochure provenance, and source-change visibility.

## Decision

Position Be-sure-ance as an IFA pre-meeting research tool that complements compareFIRST. The app must state that it does not replace compareFIRST, carrier brochures, licensed advisers, or regulated advisory workflows.

Product copy, README language, PDF briefs, share links, and bot responses should avoid advice, rankings, quotes, premiums, and recommendations.

## Rejected Approaches

- Consumer-facing comparison marketplace. Rejected because it would compete with stronger incumbents and invite quote/advice expectations.
- compareFIRST replacement framing. Rejected because it is inaccurate and politically risky.
- Carrier lead-generation or affiliate positioning. Rejected because it conflicts with public-good and trust positioning.

## Consequences

- The product has a narrower audience and clearer compliance posture.
- Public-good portfolio reviewers can understand the civic-tech angle faster.
- User acquisition depends more on IFA workflow fit than broad consumer SEO.
