# Code of Conduct

This project is a public-good, compliance-sensitive repository. Participation is expected to stay factual, respectful, and focused on verifiable improvements.

## Expected behavior

- Be direct without personal attacks.
- Assume public-sector, insurance, and compliance contexts require extra care.
- Label uncertainty when discussing data quality, legal posture, or source reliability.
- Prefer source-backed corrections over opinion.
- Respect takedown, privacy, and security concerns.

## Unacceptable behavior

- Harassment, threats, discriminatory remarks, or sexualized language.
- Publishing private credentials, personal data, client data, or non-public carrier material.
- Pressuring contributors to make unverified advice, ranking, premium, or suitability claims.
- Bypassing robots.txt, rate limits, or documented compliance boundaries.
- Sustained off-topic disruption after maintainers ask for focus.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may remove comments, close issues, reject contributions, or block accounts that violate this code. Security, privacy, compliance, and source-owner takedown concerns may be handled privately first when public discussion would increase risk.
